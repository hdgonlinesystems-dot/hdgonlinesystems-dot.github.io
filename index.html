Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions
What is the minimum project value HDG accepts?
HDG is structured for mid-to-large scale government and institutional projects. As a Class 1A CPWD contractor, we primarily engage with projects above ₹1 Crore in value. For strategic clients or multi-phase frameworks, we evaluate smaller packages on a case-by-case basis.
Which states and regions does HDG operate in?
HDG has active delivery experience across Delhi, Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Jammu & Kashmir, and Ladakh. We have executed projects in remote and extreme-altitude environments including the University of Ladakh. For pan-India requirements, we evaluate logistics and capacity on a per-project basis.
Does HDG handle both civil and MEP under the same contract?
Yes. One of HDG's core differentiators is single-contract delivery covering Civil, MEP, Interiors, and Specialized Systems. This eliminates the coordination gap between separate civil and MEP contractors that causes the majority of delays and disputes in institutional construction.
Is HDG eligible for tenders under CPWD, NBCC, and other central agencies?
Yes. HDG holds Class 1A registration with CPWD — the highest classification — making the firm eligible to tender for all central government construction contracts without a value ceiling. We have active empanelment with NBCC, DSIIDC, HSIIDC, and several other government bodies.
What documents are available for tender pre-qualification?
HDG can provide: CPWD Class 1A registration certificate, company profile, project completion certificates, ESI and PF compliance records, GST registration, PAN, audited financials, and technical capability statement. Contact us with your specific pre-qualification checklist and we will compile a complete submission package.
What is HDG's current project capacity and availability?
HDG is currently accepting new tenders for FY 2025–26. We maintain capacity for 3–5 concurrent large projects through dedicated project manager assignments and parallel workforce deployment. Contact us to discuss timelines — we'll give you an honest assessment of availability for your specific project.
Does HDG offer a site visit before tendering?
Yes. For all shortlisted projects, HDG conducts a pre-bid site assessment at no charge. This allows our team to evaluate scope, logistics, and risk factors — enabling us to submit realistic, well-costed bids rather than speculative estimates that lead to cost escalation later.
